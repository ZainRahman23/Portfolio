Pong Project

In this project, I made a modified Pong game. The first noticeable difference is the theme change into Soccer over the traditional Ice Hockey Table style. This change also altered the paddle sizes, creating unique collisions with the ball. The goal size was also shortened from the entire wall to just the visual length of the goal. Finally, I developed an AI with 3 different difficulty levels so that users can choose between single and multiplayer.
