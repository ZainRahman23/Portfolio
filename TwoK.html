Alternate 2k Ratings

Created player rankings out of 100 by regressing to find a coefficient of a real stat against 2k22 ratings of said stats and times it by the stat and adding it to another stat using the same process in the same bucket to get a value for a certain bucket. Repeated this process using the buckets created and times it by the coefficient against 2k22 total overall to get final rating
