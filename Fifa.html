Alternate Fifa Ratings

Created player rankings out of 100 by regressing to find a coefficient of a real stat against Fifa 22 ratings of said stats and times it by the stat and adding it to another stat using the same process in the same bucket to get a value for a certain bucket. Repeated this process using the buckets created and times it by the coefficient against Fifa 22 total overall to get final rating
